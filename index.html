Cavaletti Jewellery

Cape Town, South Africa.

At Cavaletti Jewellery, we specialize in creating luxury, handmade jewellery crafted from the finest gold and diamonds in South Africa. Known for sophistication and elegance, we focus on designing bespoke pieces tailored to individual tastes and special occasions.

We are passionate about creating jewellery that captures memories and brings joy to our clients. ❤

How We Bring Your Vision to Life:

  • Custom Design: We work closely with you to refine and perfect your jewellery design.
  • Material Selection: Only the finest gold, silver, gemstones, and diamonds are sourced to ensure unmatched quality.
  • Personalization: Each piece is carefully customized with attention to your unique preferences.
  • Expert Craftsmanship: Using techniques like lost-wax casting and intricate stone setting, we ensure precision and excellence in every detail.
  • Flawless Finish: Polishing and rigorous quality checks guarantee brilliance and lasting durability.
