Their pieces are known for their sophistication and elegance, with a focus on creating bespoke items for clients. The brand offers a variety of collections and custom design services tailored to individual tastes and occasions. Our Jewellry Creation Process

At Cavaletti Jewellery, each piece is crafted with precision and care, combining timeless techniques with modern innovation. Here's how we bring your vision to life:

Custom Design: From initial sketches to 3D modeling, our team works closely with you to perfect the design. Material Selection: We source only the finest gold, platinum, and diamonds for unmatched quality. Expert Craftsmanship: Using lost-wax casting, detailed stone setting, and hand-finishing, we ensure every piece meets the highest standards. Flawless Finish: Polishing and quality checks guarantee brilliance and durability.
